Admissions

Admissions are open for Playgroup to Class 8. We welcome new students into a nurturing and creative learning environment.

Admission Process:

  • Collect the admission form from the school office.
  • Submit the completed form along with required documents.
  • Attend the parent-teacher interaction or assessment (if applicable).
  • Pay the admission fee to confirm enrollment.

Documents Required:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Two Passport-size Photos
  • Previous School Report Card (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card (Student & Parent)
