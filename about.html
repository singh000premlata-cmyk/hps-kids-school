About Us

HPS Kids School, located in Etawah, Uttar Pradesh, is dedicated to nurturing young minds from Playgroup to Class 8. Our mission is to create a joyful and stimulating learning environment where every child grows with confidence and curiosity.

We combine traditional values with modern teaching methods, focusing on holistic development — academics, creativity, and moral growth.
