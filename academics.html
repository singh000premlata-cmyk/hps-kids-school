Academics

HPS Kids School offers education from Playgroup to Class 8. Our academic program is designed to provide a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and life skills.

  • Playgroup, Nursery, and Kindergarten – Early childhood development through fun learning.
  • Classes 1 to 5 – Building basics in English, Hindi, Math, EVS, and Art.
  • Classes 6 to 8 – Strengthening conceptual understanding in Science, Mathematics, Social Studies, and Languages.

Co-curricular activities include Art, Music, Sports, and Cultural Events to ensure all-round development.
